## What to Avoid
- Avoid generic AI hype commentary without a clear executive decision-making angle — the target audience (CEOs, strategy leads) needs board-level framing and quantified stakes, not surface-level trend summaries that any tech newsletter already covers.
- Avoid overly polished or abstract thought leadership that sacrifices specificity for polish — the voice analysis signals that authenticity and practitioner directness outperform curated corporate tone with this audience.
- Avoid posting without a clear call to reflection or provocation — posts that end neutrally generate minimal comment velocity, and comment density is the primary algorithmic lever for this content tier.
